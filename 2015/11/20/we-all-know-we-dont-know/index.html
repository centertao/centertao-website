We need to keep retelling our most meaningful story to keep it real because we are biologically incapable of remaining emotionally still and continuously appreciating life‚Ä¶ including the most profound experiences and realizations. Up from the ocean of emotion

In our heart of hearts, we know that we don‚Äôt know.
