Vitamin D

Recent research shows we may need up to 10 times more vitamin D than previously thought, and this makes total sense. Skin produces vitamin D from sunshine and we evolved in the very sunny environment of Africa. People who migrated north out of Africa had to evolve lighter skin to admit more sunlight to synthesize adequate vitamin D. Now we do much of our living in the shade indoors.
